THE DUEL




Can you beat the skeleton ? How many duels can you win ?


Use a,d,space to walk and jump and shift to swing your sword.
Your score is on the right side and your enemy's one on the left.


The skeleton reaction time is slow, he can miss his attacks (so can you),
but care, his attack speed is faster !


You can also try to move the mouse or press it all over the cloth
behind ! If you want to see more information about the cloth
animation visit the codepen in the link at the bottom right corner.


Use the instructions menu to edit the cloth behavoir, or
pause the game at the top left corner. (Not the cloth or the fire).


Mute or unmute the music at the bottom left corner.


Press the screen to continue !
